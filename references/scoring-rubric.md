# Scoring Rubric & Ship Gate

Score the rewrite before delivering. Extends `stop-slop`'s 5-dimension rubric with the two dimensions that
matter for scholarship: **Fidelity** and **Evidence**. Rate each 1–10.

| Dimension | Question | Fail signal |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Fidelity** *(gate)* | Every number, result, citation, term, and information point preserved? | Any altered/dropped fact, number, or cite key |
| **Evidence** *(gate)* | Does every claim earn its number/figure/citation, and no verb exceed its evidence? | Unbacked claim, or verb stronger than data |
| **Directness** | Statements, or announcements and throat-clearing? | Signposting, meta-commentary, filler openers |
| **Rhythm / Burstiness** | Uneven sentence & paragraph lengths, or metronomic? | Runs of same-length mid-length sentences |
| **Trust** | Respects reader intelligence? | Over-hedging, hand-holding, restating the obvious |
| **Authenticity** | Reads as a specific person, free of AI tells? | Em-dashes, vocab clusters, rule-of-three, hype |
| **Density** | Anything cuttable without losing information? | Redundant clauses, adjective pairs, empty summaries |

## The gate

1. **Fidelity and Evidence are hard gates.** If either scores below 8, the rewrite is **not shippable** regardless
   of style — go back to Protect/Anchor. A stylistically perfect draft that altered a number or over-claimed a
   result is a failure, not a near-miss. This is the difference between Easywrite and a generic humanizer.
2. **Sum the other five (Directness, Rhythm, Trust, Authenticity, Density).** Below **35/50** → revise (step 3–6).
3. **Then stop.** Once the two gates pass and the five clear ~40/50, ship. Further "polish" triggers the refinement
   trap (see [detector-science](detector-science.md)). Detector variance is ±10–20 points; chasing it past this point regresses.

## Quick pre-flight (30-second scan before you call it done)

- Any adverbs doing vague work? Any passive where an actor would be clearer (but keep actor-irrelevant passive)?
- An inanimate thing performing a human verb ("the complaint becomes a fix")? Name the person/system.
- Sentence starting with a Wh- word, or "here's what/this/that"? Restructure or cut.
- "not X, it's Y" contrast? State Y directly. Three consecutive sentences the same length? Break one.
- Em-dash anywhere (`—` / `–` / ` -- `)? Remove. Vague declarative ("the implications are significant")? Name it.
- **Did a number, unit, p-value, equation, or cite key change? → revert immediately.**
- **Did any verb get stronger than its evidence? → downgrade.**
